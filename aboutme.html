About me 

I graduated from the University of West London 5 years ago. Since then I have succeeded to create a flourishing career in the interior design and architecture field. I would like to work with you and transform your house into your lovely home.I have always loved interior design and architecture so everything I do, I do with love and passion. I believe that architecture is an art that not many people understand yet. The environmental space says a lot about a person but also can influence our feelings, our vibes.An interior designer is worth the money in many situations. They often add tremendous value to the project. And, they pass on some of their trade discounts too. ... They are better at getting things right the first time-such as (paint) colors- saving you time, money, and hassles.Our profession is probably one of the most stressful. We are always under some sort of pressure either by our clients or ourselves to create something that is unlike anything else done before. ... Designers who are constantly in this state are more likely not to recover their health even after leaving the profession.
